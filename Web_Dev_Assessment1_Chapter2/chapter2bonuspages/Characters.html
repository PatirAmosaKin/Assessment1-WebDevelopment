Characters

Fire Emblem: Three Houses features a rich cast of characters, each with their own unique stories and motivations. The main characters are the leaders of the three houses:

  • Black Eagles: Led by Edelgard, a strong-willed leader determined to reshape the future of Fódlan.
  • Blue Lions: Led by Dimitri, a noble and chivalrous prince with a deep sense of duty.
  • Golden Deer: Led by Claude, a charismatic and clever leader with a mysterious past.
