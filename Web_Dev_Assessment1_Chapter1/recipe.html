Cinnamon Rolls

Soft, fluffy, and irresistibly sweet, cinnamon rolls are a beloved treat perfect for breakfast, brunch, or dessert. These warm rolls are filled with a rich, spiced cinnamon-sugar filling, then topped with a creamy glaze for the ultimate indulgence. Whether enjoyed alone fresh out of the oven or paired with a coffee for a cozy morning, these homemade cinnamon rolls are guaranteed to satisfy your sweet tooth!

Ingredients for the dough

  • 4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup melted unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs

Ingredients for the filling

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup softened unsalted butter

Ingredients for the icing

  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp milk

Step-by-step Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a large mixing bowl, combine 2 cups of the flour, sugar, and salt. In a separate bowl, dissolve the yeast in the warm water and warm milk. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes, or until the mixture becomes frothy.
  2. Mix Wet and Dry Ingredients: Once the yeast mixture is frothy, add it to the flour mixture, along with the melted butter and eggs. Stir to combine. Gradually add the remaining 2 cups of flour, mixing until a dough starts to form.
  3. Knead the Dough: Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes until it’s smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean towel, and let it rise for 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.
  4. Prepare the Cinnamon Filling: In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar and cinnamon. In another small bowl, melt the softened butter. Set both aside for later.
  5. Roll Out the Dough: After the dough has risen, punch it down to release air. Turn it out onto a floured surface and roll it into a rectangle, about 16x12 inches in size.
  6. Add the Cinnamon Filling: Brush the dough with the softened butter, leaving about a 1-inch border. Evenly sprinkle the cinnamon and brown sugar mixture over the buttered dough.
  7. Roll Up the Dough: Starting from one edge, carefully roll the dough tightly into a log. Slice the rolled dough into 12 equal pieces (about 1-1.5 inches each).
  8. Prepare the Baking Pan: Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan and arrange the sliced rolls in it. Leave a little space between each roll for them to expand while baking. Cover the pan with a towel and let the rolls rise for another 30 minutes.
  9. Bake the Rolls: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the cinnamon rolls for 20-25 minutes, or until they are golden brown on top.
  10. Make the Cream Cheese Glaze: While the rolls are baking, prepare the glaze by combining the softened cream cheese, softened butter, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and milk in a bowl. Mix until smooth and creamy. Adjust the milk to reach your desired consistency.
  11. Glaze the Cinnamon Rolls: Once the rolls are out of the oven, let them cool for about 5 minutes. Drizzle the cream cheese glaze over the warm cinnamon rolls.
  12. Serve and Enjoy: Serve the cinnamon rolls warm for the best experience! Enjoy the soft, gooey goodness!
